Managerial Accounting Practices

Managerial accounting employs various techniques to enhance business performance and decision-making. Target costing sets cost goals for profitability, while activity-based costing (ABC) and time-driven ABC (TDABC) allocate costs accurately, incorporating time as a key driver. Methods like just-in-time (JIT) reduce waste, and total quality management (TQM) ensures continuous process improvement. Kaizen costing promotes incremental cost reduction, and lifecycle costing evaluates costs over a productâ€™s lifespan. Customer relationship management (CRM) focuses on customer needs to boost loyalty and revenue. Using AI to analyze data from these practices enables actionable insights and better strategies for efficiency and profitability.

Target Costing

Design the product in a way that ensures the achievement of the target cost set in advance, taking into account market requirements and customer needs, which helps in achieving a competitive price and sustainable profitability.

Activity-Based Costing - ABC

Accurate allocate costs based on resource-consuming activities, providing comprehensive insights to optimize cost and profitability strategies.

Time-Driven Activity-Based Costing - TDABC

Calculating costs based on real-time for different activities, supporting faster and more accurate decision-making to improve operational efficiency.

Just-In-Time - JIT

A production strategy based on minimizing inventory by manufacturing goods only when needed, which reduces waste and enhances efficiency in the supply chain.

Total Quality Management - TQM

Continuous quality improvement at all stages of production and services, ensuring exceptional customer value and sustainable customer satisfaction.

Kaizen Costing

Focus on gradual and continuous improvement in production processes, leading to reduced costs and enhanced overall performance.

Lifecycle Costing

Analyze product-related costs throughout its entire lifecycle, from design to disposal, to ensure strategic decisions are made that support sustainability and efficiency.

Customer Relationship Management - CRM

Build strong customer relationships through data management and personalization of experiences, boosting loyalty and contributing to higher revenue.
